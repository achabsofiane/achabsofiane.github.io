Osme as Founder

Osme offers an ethical and creative alternative in fashion by supporting emerging fashion designers in the development of their brand. This is done through a digital platform and pop-up concept stores.

Design & osme.fr platform development

User research, creation of a concept diverging from traditional e-commerce norms to suit a specific product in the fashion industry. Prototyping, segmentation to define an MVP, and platform development.

The concept: applying the principles of art to fashion to emphasize the creative process and craftsmanship that lie behind a designer's piece.

The landing page

A phygital experience bridging the gap between the Osme platform and the concept store.

The home page

A fresh perspective on e-commerce.

To know more about Osme…

The vision

The price of a piece should be justified by the craftsmanship and creativity of the designer, not by the brand's reputation. A new shopping experience should be offered by encouraging the customer to engage with the story and craftsmanship behind the piece.

The solutions

An e-commerce platform alone is not enough to convey the artistic sensitivity and craftsmanship of the designers.

1 digital marketplace ; 4 concept-stores ; 70+ brands

The marketplace

Provide a digital presence for designers, with a space that goes beyond just purchasing and an online store, but also includes a discovery area to enhance the customer experience.

The concept-store

Create a space to exhibit designer pieces. A meeting place where consumers and designers can engage in discussions about craftsmanship and shared values.
